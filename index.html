Citizen Service


Welcome to Citizen Service, a community-driven platform that connects residents with local government to ensure faster, smarter infrastructure maintenance. Here, you can easily report issues such as potholes, broken streetlights, water leaks, or other public service faults by submitting a simple form or photo. Our AI-powered system helps categorize and prioritize reports, ensuring they reach the right department quickly. You’ll also receive timely feedback and updates on your submissions, making service delivery more transparent and accountable. Together, we can build safer, cleaner, and more reliable communities.

Amazing Services & Features for you

The website has one of the best services and features as stated below

Real-Time Tracking & Feedback

Every report gets a case number, and residents can track its progress from submitted to resolved. Push notifications, SMS, or email updates keep the community informed.

Community Dashboard & Transparency

Public-facing dashboard showing reported issues, their status, and resolved cases. Encourages accountability and builds trust between citizens and government.

Smart Fault Reporting

Residents can report issues by filling out a quick form, attaching photos, or using voice input. AI automatically categorizes the issue (pothole, streetlight, water leak, etc.) for faster processing..

AI-Powered Insights for Government

Automatic routing of reports to the correct department. Predictive analytics to identify problem hotspots and plan preventive maintenance.
